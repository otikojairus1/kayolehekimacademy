About us

Get to know us more!!

Kayole Hekima Academy is a learning institution registered with the Ministry of Education under number PE/9322/13. This institution was founded in the year 2000 and currently has 300 pupils, 16 teachers and 8 non-teaching staff. Moreover, the school is found in Kayole Soweto area and sits on its own piece of land measuring 0.178ha.this is a slum area where income srae very low posing challenges in the payment of school fees by parents thus the school has to support 100 pupils coming from needy families in terms of feeding programs and educational utilities.

In addition the school is a registered examinational center with the Kenya national examination council for Kenya certificate of primary education.
